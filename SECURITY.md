# Security Policy

## English (Primary)

### Supported Scope
This policy applies to repositories and systems governed by the NOIRFEATHER holding.

### Reporting a Vulnerability
Send a confidential report to the repository owner and include:
- Affected component and entity.
- Reproduction steps.
- Impact assessment.
- Proof of concept or evidence.

Do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly before coordinated remediation is complete.

### Response Targets
- Initial acknowledgement: within 3 business days.
- Triage decision: within 7 business days.
- Remediation timeline: based on severity and legal constraints.

### Severity Model
- Critical: active exploitation or severe business/safety impact.
- High: significant compromise of confidentiality, integrity, or availability.
- Medium: limited impact with viable mitigations.
- Low: minimal impact or informational issue.
